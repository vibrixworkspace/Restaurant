Our Story

A Culinary Journey
Born in Coimbatore

Nestled in the heart of Coimbatore, Kadhambam is more than just a restaurant — it's an experience crafted with warmth, tradition, and the finest flavours of India. Our Chettinad-inspired interiors and luxurious ambience make every meal a celebration.

Famous for our legendary biryanis, aromatic Chettinad specialties, sizzling tandoori platters, and comforting family meals — Kadhambam blends the heritage of South Indian cooking with the richness of multi-cuisine excellence.
